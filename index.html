The 3-Step Method

How every question teaches you to think

01

Spot the Time Marker

Scan the sentence for clue words. "last year" anchors you in the past. "right now" signals the present continuous.

Example clue "...when he was in London last year?"
02

Identify the Tense Family

Match the clue to its tense category. Past markers → Past Simple. Ongoing action markers → Present Continuous. Habitual facts → Present Simple.

Past Simple Present Simple Continuous Future Simple
03

Select the Correct Verb Form

With the tense locked in, apply the rule: questions revert to base form, negatives use auxiliary verbs. Accuracy becomes logical, not lucky.

does / live did / live is / living
